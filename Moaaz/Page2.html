This is a fruit birthday cake made with soft cream and pink cream. It is topped with strawberries, mango slices, cherries, and grapes, with sprinkles on top.

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Pink cream
  • Strawberries
  • Mango slices
  • Cherries
  • Grapes
  • Vanilla sponge cake

How to Make It

  1. Bake a vanilla cake and let it cool.
  2. Cover the cake with cream.
  3. Add pink cream on top.
  4. Add fruits: strawberries, mango, cherries, grapes.
  5. Add sprinkles.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
