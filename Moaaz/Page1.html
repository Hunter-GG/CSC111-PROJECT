Today's Special: Pancakes ü•û

Pancakes are soft, fluffy, and easy to make. They are perfect for breakfast or a sweet snack. You can serve them with honey, chocolate, or fruits.

Ingredients Checklist

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 spoons of sugar
  • 1 spoon of oil
  • Honey or chocolate for topping

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, milk, and one egg.
  2. Stir until the batter becomes smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour a small amount of the mixture.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.
  5. Serve with honey or chocolate syrup.
